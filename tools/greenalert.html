South African law says planned development projects, including mines, dams, power stations, roads and landfill sites, need to have their environmental impacts assessed before they can go ahead. #GreenAlert helps you to find out what Environmental Impact Assessments (EIAs) are happening in your area.

Find your location to see the details of an EIA: its official ID, the project description, status of the development, and the government body responsible for authorizing and monitoring the development.
