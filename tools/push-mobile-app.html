Push Mobile App

Android and iOS app for news organizations that don’t have the time, money or resources to build their own custom news apps.

Push is an open-source Android and iOS mobile app for news agencies and publications that don’t have the time, money or resources to build their own custom code base for an app. The app lets users view the publisher's newest stories and search for keywords in old articles. News outlets also can use the app to push new content directly to their users' phone lock screens. The app includes a feature for news organizations to run fundraising campaigns and collect donations from users through PayPal. Very soon, it will have the ability to send push notifications to users and host Facebook Live broadcasts. ICFJ Knight Fellow Chris Guess has used Push to create mobile apps for OCCRP (Android / iOS), Meydan TV (Android / iOS) and Rise Moldova (Android / iOS). For more: http://bit.ly/1LqBV01
